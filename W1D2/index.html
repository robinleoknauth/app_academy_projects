Glorious Breakfast Burrito

Really delicious burrito with cheese and sausage.

Picture of Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Sausage
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Heat tortillas until hot
  3. Chop up tomatoes and sausage
  4. Cook Sausage
  5. Take out tortillas
  6. Put food in tortilla
